    Huiteng Unbrella CO., LTD

    HUITENG UMBRELLA CO., LTD was founded in 2012, the headquarter is in Quanzhou, China. HuiTeng aims to provide high quality products with high production efficiency and best services. Currently, there are two production lines and more than 200 employees at Huiteng. The products have been exported to many countries including the US, the UK, Japan, Italy, Germany and etc.

    Based on continuous innovation, the company has gained high reputation and popularity among customers and vendors, it has been placed a leading position in the umbrella industry.

    The company has a wide range of products including regular umbrella, with anti-wind proof function umbrella, auto open/close umbrella, kid’s umbrella, golf umbrella, POE transparent umbrella.
